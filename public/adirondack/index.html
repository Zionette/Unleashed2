Passion. Encourage. Opportunity. Power. Love. Energy. (P.E.O.P.L.E) To heal the earth and bring global community back to the planet. Building sustainable villages by the means of Permaculture. Permaculture is about designing sustainable human settlements. It is a philosophy and an approach to land use which weaves together micro-climate, annual and perennial plants, animals, soils, water management, and human needs into intricately connected productive communities.
