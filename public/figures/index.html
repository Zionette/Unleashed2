One Community is a 501(c)3 non-profit think tank. We are a 100%-volunteer organization and our mission is to help people create a better world by demonstrating a more sustainable and enriching way of living and open source everything needed for replication. This includes free-shared and “Highest Good” approaches to food, energy, housing, education, for-profit and non-profit economic design, social architecture, fulfilled living, global stewardship practices, and more. Combined, we will use these to build One Community as the first of many teacher/demonstration hubs. These hubs will form a global collaborative to provide even more research, blueprints, and necessary support for successively easier, more affordable, and creative duplication everywhere.

SKILLS EVERY MEMBER OF OUR TEAM HAS

Every member of the One Community team has a foundational set of skills that help us keep moving forward in a way that is effective, efficient, and enjoyable. Anyone interested in joining our team should exemplify these as well.

  • Charismatic and fun personality
  • Self-motivation and accountability
  • Dependability and a strong work ethic
  • Excellent and timely communication skills
  • Attention to detail and dedication to quality
